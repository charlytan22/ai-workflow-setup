# Ross Simmonds - AI-Proof Content and Content Distribution


## Key Ideas

### 1. AI-Proof Content

Ross argues that businesses should focus on content that AI cannot easily replicate.

Examples include:

- Original research
- Customer insights
- Internal company data
- Unique experiences
- Expert opinions

Generic summaries are increasingly vulnerable to commoditization.

### 2. Distribution Is the Competitive Advantage

Many companies focus on creating content but neglect distribution.

Ross emphasizes:

- Repurposing content
- Multi-channel publishing
- Community engagement
- Strategic amplification

### 3. Content Is a Long-Term Asset

Content should be treated as an asset that can be continuously repurposed.

Examples:

- Blog → LinkedIn posts
- Webinar → Articles
- Research report → Newsletter
- Podcast → Social content

### 4. Brand Building Matters More Than Ever

As AI-generated content increases, recognizable brands gain an advantage because users and AI systems trust established authorities.

---

## Strategic Implications for B2B SaaS

- Invest in proprietary research.
- Build unique viewpoints.
- Create content distribution systems.
- Prioritize brand authority over content volume.

## Key Takeaway

The winning strategy is not producing the most content. The winning strategy is producing unique content and distributing it more effectively than competitors.
