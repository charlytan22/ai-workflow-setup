# Mike King - Relevance Engineering and the Future of Search

## Key Ideas

### 1. Search Has Become Semantic

Search engines no longer operate primarily through keyword matching.

Modern search systems evaluate:

- Meaning
- Context
- Relationships between concepts
- Passage-level relevance

### 2. Relevance Engineering

Mike King argues that SEO professionals should act more like engineers than content publishers.

This involves:

- Understanding retrieval systems
- Studying AI search behavior
- Structuring information for machine understanding
- Building content ecosystems instead of individual pages

### 3. Visibility Is Replacing Rankings

As AI Overviews become more common, traditional ranking reports become less meaningful.

Important metrics now include:

- Brand visibility
- Mentions
- Citations
- Presence inside AI-generated answers

### 4. Content Must Become Multi-Format

AI systems learn from multiple content types.

Future SEO requires:

- Articles
- Research papers
- Videos
- Community discussions
- Thought leadership content

---

## Strategic Implications for B2B SaaS

- Invest in topical authority.
- Create machine-readable content structures.
- Produce original research.
- Build visibility beyond traditional search results.

## Key Takeaway

SEO is evolving from keyword optimization into relevance engineering, where the goal is helping AI systems understand, trust, and surface your content.
