Summary: Andy Crestodina discusses using AI as a content analyst to identify the most "clickable" and "engaging" parts of content. He posits that AI is more effective as an editor and analyst than as a primary writer.

Key Takeaways:

• AI excels at analyzing existing content to pinpoint elements that drive engagement, such as compelling quotes, soundbites, or statistics.

• Instead of relying on AI for initial content generation, leverage it to refine and optimize human-created content for better performance.

• AI can be "trained to be a sales rep" by feeding it high-performing sales copy and then asking it to apply those patterns to improve other marketing materials.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
B2B SaaS companies can integrate AI into their content review and optimization processes. This involves using AI to analyze content for engagement potential, identify areas for improvement, and ensure that the content aligns with conversion goals. The focus is on augmenting human creativity and strategic thinking with AI-driven analytical power to produce highly effective SEO content.
