Summary: Mike King's concept of "Relevance Engineering" explains how AI Overviews (AIOs) and search agents source answers from diverse platforms like LinkedIn, YouTube, and Quora. He emphasizes the need for technical optimization to ensure content is accessible and relevant to LLM retrieval systems.

Key Takeaways:

• AI Overviews are changing how information is consumed, drawing from a wider range of sources beyond traditional websites.

• "Relevance Engineering" involves making websites technically accessible and highly relevant to Large Language Model (LLM) retrieval systems.

• Structured data, particularly Schema.org, is more critical than ever for effectively communicating content meaning to LLMs.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
B2B SaaS companies must prioritize technical SEO and structured data implementation to ensure their content is not only discoverable by traditional search engines but also understandable and citable by AI. This means going beyond basic on-page SEO to focus on the underlying technical architecture that facilitates AI comprehension and retrieval.
