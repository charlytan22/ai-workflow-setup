Summary: Chima Mmeje discusses building story-led "how-to" content from events and webinars using AI, specifically for SaaS companies. Her approach focuses on leveraging AI to maintain an "expert voice" while transforming raw content into various formats.

Key Takeaways:

• AI can be effectively used to repurpose content from webinars and events into diverse formats like LinkedIn posts, blog articles, and newsletters.

• The key is to maintain the original "expert voice" and narrative throughout the AI-assisted content transformation process.

• Human-in-the-loop (HITL ) workflows are crucial, where AI handles the initial drafting and heavy lifting, and human experts provide the final unique narrative and polish.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
For B2B SaaS, this highlights an efficient strategy for maximizing content ROI. By using AI to scale content repurposing, companies can extend the reach and impact of their expert-led content. The emphasis on HITL ensures that the content remains authentic and aligned with the brand's expertise, which is vital for building trust and authority in AI-driven search environments.
