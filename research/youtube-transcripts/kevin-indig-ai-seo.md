# Kevin Indig - AI Search Optimization and the Future of SEO

## Key Ideas

### 1. AI Overviews Reduce Clicks

Traditional search behavior is changing because users can now receive answers directly from AI-generated summaries.

Even when websites are cited, click-through rates tend to be significantly lower than traditional organic listings. Informational and top-of-funnel content is most affected. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

### 2. Measure Visibility, Not Just Rankings

Future SEO metrics should include:

- Brand mentions
- AI citations
- Referral traffic from AI platforms
- Share of voice across AI search systems

### 3. Build Authority Across Multiple Platforms

AI systems increasingly pull information from:

- Websites
- YouTube
- Reddit
- Forums
- Industry publications

---

## Strategic Implications for B2B SaaS

- Optimize for AI citations, not just rankings.
- Build recognizable brand authority.
- Publish original research and proprietary insights.
- Expand beyond blog content into multimedia and community channels.

## Key Takeaway

The future of SEO is not winning Position #1. The future of SEO is becoming the source that AI systems trust and reference.
