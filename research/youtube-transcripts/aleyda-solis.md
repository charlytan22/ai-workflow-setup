Title: Leverage AI to support & accelerate SEO workflows
Date: May 2024 - 2025
Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NWvaYwSMVrc

Summary: Aleyda Solis demonstrates how AI can be leveraged to support and accelerate various SEO workflows beyond just content generation. She highlights the use of AI for technical audits, keyword clustering, and data analysis, freeing up human SEOs for more strategic tasks.

Frameworks Mentioned:

• AI-powered SEO Workflow Automation: Utilizing AI to automate repetitive and data-intensive SEO tasks, allowing human experts to focus on higher-level strategy and analysis.

Actionable Lessons:

• AI can automate "boring" but essential SEO tasks such as mapping redirects, analyzing log files, and performing initial technical audits.

• Custom GPTs and AI agents can be developed for specific SEO functions, like conducting competitor content gap analysis or generating keyword clusters.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
For B2B SaaS, this means integrating AI into the operational aspects of SEO to enhance efficiency and accuracy. By automating routine tasks, teams can allocate more resources to strategic content planning, creative execution, and in-depth analysis, ultimately leading to more effective AI-powered SEO content production. This approach emphasizes AI as a powerful assistant for SEO professionals, not a replacement.
