Title: How to Rank SEO Content in the Era of Generative AI
Date: 2024-2025


Summary: Bernard Huang, co-founder of Clearscope, emphasizes "Information Gain" as the crucial ranking factor in the age of AI. He argues that content merely reiterating existing information will not be cited by AI and thus will not rank effectively.

Frameworks Mentioned:

• Information Gain: The concept that content must provide new, unique, or significantly deeper insights than what is already available to be valuable to search engines and AI.

Actionable Lessons:

• To achieve information gain, content creators should integrate proprietary data, present contrarian viewpoints, or include expert quotes that are not widely published.

• While optimizing for search intent is important, prioritizing user experience and establishing trust signals are equally vital for AI-driven search.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
B2B SaaS companies must move beyond basic keyword optimization and focus on creating truly insightful and unique content. AI tools can assist in identifying gaps in existing content and suggesting areas for deeper exploration, but human expertise is essential for generating the novel insights that drive information gain and AI citation.
