Summary: Kevin Indig's Growth Memo outlines how search is evolving from traditional ranked lists to definitive answers provided by AI. He emphasizes the new importance of retrieval, citation, and trust factors in this AI-driven search landscape.

Key Takeaways:

• Traditional SEO metrics are becoming less relevant; "Brand Mentions" in AI answers are a more significant indicator of visibility.

• The "AEO/GEO Optimization Loop" involves continuously monitoring, optimizing, and measuring a brand's presence within Large Language Models (LLMs ).

• Real-time optimization guidance for both traditional SEO and AI search visibility is crucial for adapting to these changes.

How This Applies To AI-Powered SEO Content Production:
This highlights the necessity for B2B SaaS companies to shift their SEO strategies to account for Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). Content needs to be structured and authoritative enough to be cited by AI, and brands must actively track their presence within AI-generated answers. The focus moves beyond keywords to comprehensive, trustworthy information that AI can readily use.
